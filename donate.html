Your Impact

Where your donation goes

LabLink Initiative has no seed funding and no operating budget. Every program we run, every consultation we offer, and every resource we produce is powered by people who believe in what we're building. Here's what your support directly funds:

🎓

Student Consultations

Free 1:1 advising sessions that walk students through national lab and federal internship applications — the process most advisors don't know.

📬

The LinkBot Newsletter

Monthly deep-tech publications reaching 5,000+ students with career pathways in quantum computing, clean energy, biotech, and more.

🔬

CDP Platform Development

Hosting, development, and iteration on our AI-powered skill gap assessment and internship matching platform.

🏆

Scholarships & Grants

Direct financial support to students who need it most — with goals to reach $50K+ in annual awards within 5 years.

🤝

Events & Workshops

Virtual and in-person events that connect students directly with national lab professionals and federal program managers.
