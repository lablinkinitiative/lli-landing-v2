STEM Bridge Programs: Connecting High Schoolers to Real Science

The gap between classroom science and real research is enormous — and it's not random who falls into it. Students who go on to careers in STEM typically share something in common: at some point, someone gave them access to a real lab, a real experiment, or a real researcher.
