1. Overview

LabLink Initiative ("LLI," "we," "us," or "our") is a 501(c)(3) nonprofit organization. We are committed to protecting your personal information and being transparent about how we use it.

This Privacy Policy applies to lablinkinitiative.org, cdp.lablinkinitiative.org, intern.lablinkinitiative.org, newsletter.lablinkinitiative.org, and any other platforms we operate. By using our services, you agree to the practices described in this policy.

The short version: We collect only what we need to provide our services. We do not sell, rent, or share your personal data with advertisers or data brokers.

2. Information We Collect

Information you provide directly:

  • Account information — name, email address, and password when you register for the CDP.
  • Profile information — academic background, career interests, skills, and related information you choose to enter in the Career Development Platform.
  • Resume data — if you upload a resume, we extract and store the text content to power career pathway and gap analysis features.
  • Communications — emails or messages you send us directly.
  • Newsletter sign-ups — your email address and name if you subscribe to our newsletter.

Information collected automatically:

  • Usage data — pages visited, features used, and interactions with our platforms (collected in server logs).
  • Device and browser information — browser type, operating system, and IP address.

Information from third parties:

  • Google OAuth — if you sign in with Google, we receive your name, email address, and Google account ID from Google. We do not receive your Google password.

3. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Create and manage your account on our platforms.
  • Provide career pathway analysis, program recommendations, and gap analysis features in the CDP.
  • Send you the newsletter and program opportunity updates you subscribe to.
  • Respond to your questions and support requests.
  • Improve our services and understand how students use our platforms.
  • Fulfill our nonprofit mission of connecting students with career opportunities.

We do not use your information for advertising, profiling for commercial purposes, or any purpose unrelated to our mission.

4. Information Sharing

We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal information. We share information only in the following limited circumstances:

  • Service providers — we use third-party services such as Vercel (website hosting), Google (OAuth sign-in), and email delivery services. These providers process data on our behalf and are bound by confidentiality obligations.
  • Legal requirements — we may disclose information if required by law, court order, or to protect the rights, property, or safety of LLI, our users, or the public.
  • Organizational transfers — if LLI merges with or transfers operations to another nonprofit organization, your information may transfer as part of that transaction, subject to equivalent privacy protections.

We never share your resume, profile data, or personal information with program sponsors, internship hosts, or other third parties without your explicit consent.

5. Cookies and Tracking

Our platforms use minimal cookies and local browser storage:

  • Authentication tokens — stored in your browser's local storage to keep you signed in to the CDP.
  • Session preferences — small pieces of data to remember your settings and preferences.

We do not use third-party advertising cookies, tracking pixels, or behavioral analytics tools. We do not participate in cross-site tracking.

You can clear your browser's local storage at any time through your browser settings, which will sign you out of our platforms.

6. Data Retention

We retain your information for as long as your account is active or as needed to provide our services. If you request account deletion, we will delete your personal information within 30 days, except where retention is required by law or for legitimate operational purposes (such as fraud prevention).

Newsletter subscribers who unsubscribe will be removed from our mailing list promptly.

7. Security

We take reasonable technical and organizational measures to protect your information, including:

  • HTTPS encryption for all data in transit.
  • Hashed and salted password storage (we never store passwords in plain text).
  • JWT-based authentication with expiring tokens.
  • Nightly encrypted database backups.

No method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure. While we work hard to protect your information, we cannot guarantee absolute security. If you believe your account has been compromised, please contact us immediately at lablinkinitiative@gmail.com.

8. Your Rights

You have the following rights regarding your personal information:

  • Access — you may request a copy of the personal information we hold about you.
  • Correction — you may update or correct your information through your account profile, or by contacting us.
  • Deletion — you may request that we delete your account and associated personal data.
  • Portability — you may request an export of your CDP profile data.
  • Unsubscribe — you may unsubscribe from our newsletter at any time using the link in any email we send.

To exercise any of these rights, contact us at lablinkinitiative@gmail.com. We will respond within 30 days.

9. Children's Privacy

Our services are not directed to children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe we have inadvertently collected information from a child under 13, please contact us immediately and we will delete it.

Users between 13 and 18 should have a parent or guardian review this Privacy Policy before creating an account.

10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. When we do, we will update the "Last Updated" date at the top of this page. For significant changes, we will make reasonable efforts to notify registered users by email or through a notice on our website.

Your continued use of our services after changes are posted constitutes your acceptance of the revised policy.

11. Contact Us

If you have questions, concerns, or requests related to this Privacy Policy or your personal data, please contact us:

  • Email: lablinkinitiative@gmail.com
  • Organization: LabLink Initiative (501(c)(3) Nonprofit)

We take privacy concerns seriously and will respond to all inquiries within 30 days.
