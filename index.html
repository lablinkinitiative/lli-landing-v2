Our Mission

Bridging the gap between campus and career.

LabLink Initiative educates and empowers college-aged students by providing relevant career experiences. We connect students with valuable opportunities in STEM and Business — through partnerships with National Labs, federal agencies, industry leaders, and academic institutions.

We were built by lab alumni for students who want to do meaningful work. Whether your goal is a DOE National Laboratory, a Fortune 500 company, a startup, a federal agency, or graduate school — we're here to guide you.

"The gap between what students learn and what careers require is real. We close it."
