Our Mission

Bridging the gap between campus and career.

5K+ newsletter subscribers
25+ partner organizations
100% free for students

LabLink Initiative educates and empowers college-aged students by providing relevant career experiences. We connect students with valuable opportunities in STEM and Business — through partnerships with National Labs, federal agencies, industry leaders, and academic institutions.

We were built by lab alumni for students who want to do meaningful work. Whether your goal is a DOE National Laboratory, a Fortune 500 company, a startup, a federal agency, or graduate school — we're here to guide you.

"The gap between what students learn and what careers require is real. We close it."

Built by lab alumni, for the next generation.

Our co-founders went from community college to DOE National Labs to leading research institutions. They built LabLink so the path they took is available to everyone.

Meet the founders

Not a product.
A community.

Most career resources point students at a job board and call it done. We do something different: we sit with students, understand where they want to go, and help build the path to get there — whether that's a national lab, a federal agency, a startup, or graduate school.

We're a nonprofit. We don't charge students. We don't take placement fees. We work because we believe the talent exists — it just needs connection, guidance, and access.
